WELCOME!

Portland Field Guide is here to help you familiarize yourself with the local flora in Portland, OR. This app was created to enable you to become an expert at recognizing plants and trees when you are out exploring the many urban green spaces the city has to offer.

You can click on the sections below to explore native species & plant communities, see which common plants are actually invasive, and checkout our handy tips on how to start identifying plants. Peruse the glossary of terms to learn about the plant-related vocabulary you'll see around our site. Check out the search page for sort through out databse of plants.

Then you're ready to head out into the green spaces of Portland and start identifying!
